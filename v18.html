Maison Vertige

Five fragrances. Forty years apart.

Description

Parisian fragrance house, established 1984. One address. No ambassadors, no explanations. A single numbered composition issued each decade. Five specimens recorded. Collection complete.

Specimens

i. Vertige N° 1 1984
ii. Vertige N° 2 1994
iii. Vertige N° 3 2004
iv. Vertige N° 4 2014
v. Vertige N° 5 2024
Paris, France Est. 1984
