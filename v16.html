Maison Vertige
Paris
Spring, 2024

Dear friend,

We do not advertise. We have never employed an ambassador, nor offered an explanation. Since 1984, this house has operated from a single address in Paris with a single conviction: one fragrance per decade, nothing more.

Five now exist in the world:

  • Vertige N° 1 — 1984
  • Vertige N° 2 — 1994
  • Vertige N° 3 — 2004
  • Vertige N° 4 — 2014
  • Vertige N° 5 — 2024

There will not be a sixth. Five fragrances, forty years apart. That is the whole of it.

With regards,

Maison Vertige

P.S. — If you are reading this, someone thought you should.
